What is Self Care?

Self care is anything you do to take care of yourself so you can stay physically, mentally, and emotionally well!

A few years ago I was looking for ways to practice mindfulness in a more efficient way to prevent burn out from full-time school assignments while working and taking on an internship. Learning about self-care has allowed me to manage and learn about myself better, not only in a physical sense but in other realms too, such as mentally and emotionally.
